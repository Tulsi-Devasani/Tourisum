Charming Europe(Land Only)

Day 1 - Arrive Paris

Arrive in Paris – one of the most popular cities of Europe, replete with gorgeous architecture, fashion and food. Once you arrive at the airport, make your way to the hotel Spend the day relaxing and taking in the breath-taking sights this colourful city has to offer Overnight stay at the hotel

Day 2 - Explore Paris - City Tour with Eiffel Tower and Seine River Cruise

After a lip-smacking breakfast at the hotel, get ready to experience the energy of Paris Begin your discovery of Paris with a city Hop-on Hop-off tour bus. Today, you’ll explore the French capital with a Paris City Tour on Seat in coach basis. You will visit Arc de Triomphe, Notre Dame Cathedral and Concorde Square, Opera Garnier. You will also visit the 2nd level of the legendary Eiffel Tower. In the evening, venture out in the Latin Quarter, which is renowned for its lively atmosphere and French “bistros.” or admire the most beautiful views of Paris, take in the very best view of the Eiffel Tower. You will head for a cruise on Seine River and enjoy the views of the city while cruising Overnight stay at hotel

Day 3 - Paris

Good morning! Breakfast is ready and will be served at the hotel Spend the rest of the day in the fashion capital of Europe at leisure. Spend an unforgettable time of your life meeting all the well known and loved Disney characters to experience the thrill of the rides at Disneyland Paris. Embark on a truly magical adventure, where all classic attractions and colourful shows bring limitless imagination to life. The streets are filled with wonder as beloved Disney characters glide past whist singing and dancing in the parade. Overnight stay at hotel

Day 4 - Paris - Brussels

After a lovely breakfast at the hotel, prepare for the next leg of your Europe tour Check-out from your hotel and make your way to the Paris Train Station for your train to Brussels (Train prices not included, available on request) Arrive Brussels, transfer to hotel. Check-in at the hotel and relax today Overnight stay at the hotel

Day 5 - Explore Brussels - City Tour

Enjoy a delicious breakfast at the hotel Today, you will experience Brussels in a Hop On Hop Off Tour of the city. Brussels is home to the European Union with the European Parliament, Manneken Pis, Cinquantenaire Arch, Art Nouveau and Victor Horta, the Grand-Place and the Atomium. The city is rich in architectural monuments, from the Renaissance to modern times and truly full of surprises Overnight stay at hotel

Day 6 - Brussels - Amsterdam

Enjoy a scrumptious breakfast at the hotel It’s time to Check-out from the hotel and make your way to the train station for the next destination in your tour! Take the train to Amsterdam (Train prices not included, available on request) Arrive Amsterdam. Transfer from the train station to the hotel. Check-in at the hotel. Spend the day unwinding and relaxing Overnight stay at the hotel

Day 7 - Explore Amsterdam - City Tour

Wake up and grab a delicious breakfast at the hotel Head to your Amsterdam city tour with Canal Boat and Heineken beer factory visit on seat in coach basis. Tour to explore the vibrant city. This bustling capital of Netherlands never ceases to disappoint. This tour allows you stop near top attractions, historical museums, monuments and of course the distinctive canal belt of Amsterdam. Don’t miss delights like the Van Gogh museum, the Heineken Brewery and the Flower Market. Overnight stay at hotel

Day 8 - Departure

All great holidays must end. Relish a yummy breakfast at the hotel and pack your bags and memories Check-out from the hotel and make your way to the airport for your onward flight.
