Arrival in the Netherlands

Day 1 - Arrival in the Netherlands

Welcome to Paris: Tour Manager / Local Representative will meet you outside the airport. Proceed for dinner and then to the hotel and check–in Note: Joining direct passenger / NRI can check-in on your own at the hotel. The standard check-in time is 1500 hrs. Packed dinner will be provided in case of late arrival of flight or if there is a long queue at the immigration.

Day 2 -Guided city tour of Paris – Eiffel Tower (2nd Level):

Enjoy a guided city tour: See the Alexander Bridge, Arc de Triumph, Concorde Square, Opera House, Invalides, Champs Elysees and much more. Next Visit the Eiffel Tower (2nd level). Tonight you have a chance to see the world famous Lido Show. Recommended Optional Activity - Lido Show (At an Additional Cost)

Day 3 - Onto Brussels!

Drive to Brussels for an orientation tour: Visit the Grand Place and see the Manneken Pis statue

Day 4 - Keukenhof (till 9th May) OR Madurodam (from 10th May):

Visit Keukenhof (till 9th May) OR Visit Madurodam (from 10th May). Enjoy Free time at Dam Square or you have a choice to join see Amsterdam on a canal cruise followed by a visit to one of the oldest and largest Breweries – Heineken Brewery for the Heineken experience. Recommended Optional Activity - Heineken Brewery and Amsterdam Canal Cruise (At an Additional Cost)

Day 5 - Free Time at Cologne - Rhine River cruise:

Proceed to Cologne and enjoy free time. Later enjoy a scenic cruise on the River Rhine. Proceed to Franfurt for a guided city tour

Day 6 - Fly back home with pleasant memories:

Proceed to the airport for your flight back home. Do spare a few minutes to share your feedback with your Tour Manager. Your valued suggestions will be most appreciated. Arrive home with pleasant memories of your European tour, which we are sure you will treasure for a lifetime
