Highlights Of Europe (Holiday Saving Account)

Day 1 - Welcome to London.

The Tour Manager / Station Manager will meet you outside the airport. Proceed for dinner and then to the hotel and check–in. Note: Joining direct passenger / NRI can check-in on your own at the hotel. The standard check-in time is 1500 hrs. Packed dinner will be provided in case of late arrivals of flight or long queue at the immigration.

Day 2 -Guided city tour of London:

The Buckingham Palace, Hyde Park, Big Ben, Westminster Abbey, Houses of Parliament, Trafalgar Square, Tower Bridge and Thames River. Visit the famous Madame Tussauds wax museum. Enjoy a ride on the London Eye.

Day 3 - Amsterdam:

Today, after breakfast at the hotel enjoy the day at leisure OR you can opt for Day trip to Zaanse Schans Windmills and Volendam in a Small-Group Tour. Get the full experience of all the Dutch countryside has to offer on this tour from Amsterdam. Experience an iconic landscape of fishing villages, windmills, and craftsmen’s.

Day 4 - Amsterdam:

Today, after breakfast at the hotel enjoy the day at leisure OR you can opt for One hour Evening Canal Cruise excursion in a small group tour. In evening cruise you will explore Amsterdam, its famous landmarks, culture, and rich trading history whilst relaxing on our (covered) 100% electric luxury boat.

Day 5 - Amsterdam:

Today, after breakfast, board your private van and proceed to the airport for your flight home. We hope you take back many happy memories of your holiday. We would like you to write and tell us how you enjoyed your holiday and give us your suggestions.
